Custom Video Player

A smart Learning Management System(LMS) especially created for educational institution's which will provide Administration, Video & Written Lecture's and the communication through messaging. The Website consist of both Teacher and Student's panel and provided by a student and teacher profiles. This LMS is built using Node.js Express.js and MongoDB for the Database and the Socket.io is used for the Real-time communication.
